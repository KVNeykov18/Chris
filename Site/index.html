Why the name Pozdrav?

We wanted our name to be something everyone understood and liked. At the same time to have some other meaning which not all of us think about when using the word...

It is the meaning "По-здрав", or also said in English: Healthier.

This meaning is important because without keeping our marine life alive and wiithout caring about it, we aren't healthy ourselves. We stop thinking outside the box and live a really grey life.
