What is this project about?

This is a project about the Economy of the water and the air, images of what our generation sees.

Most of us may not see it, but just one person can make a change. Just one ciggarate that wasn't left on the beach but thrown in a bin makes a difference. Just one filther in a factory makes a difference.

Here in this little online gallery, we would like to show you what the marine life can actually be if our generations combine powers and try to change the disappearing resources that the planted is giving us. Images before and after the destruction we caused not only in the oceans but also in the seas, rivers, lakes, etc.

Are the Blue and Green Economy related? Can I help with both of them?

Fortunatley, when we help, for example, the plants we also help the air, we stop the climate change little by little, because helping the enviorment isn't one big step, its small but important steps. By having more filters in factories we also help the marine life, because so much toxic waste goes in the water. Not only it kills the fish but we are also convinced that the water is actually drinkable!
